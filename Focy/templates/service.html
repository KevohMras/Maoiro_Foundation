Services

Maoiro Foundation has over the years offered these services to different communities across Kenya:

Clean Water

We have sunk several boreholes in localities that lacked clean water as an essential basic need. This project has impacted alot of families and in the process, also created several jobs for the locals.

Youth Engagement

We often host youth bootcamps to listen to the issues they face, offer counselling to them and also advice on potential business opportunities they might venture in so as to earn a living for themselves.

Quality Education

We have setup several schools within the localities that host us. Quality education is a fundamental thing if we are to bring positive change to a community or a country at large.

Proposed additional Rehab Center

To cope with growing number of drug addicts in our already established facilities, we ought to setup a new center to avoid the available facilities being overwhelmed.
